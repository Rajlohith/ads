MABlytic — PWA Icon Generator

Click any icon to select it, then download all required sizes for your PWA.

How to use:
1. Click "Download All Sizes" on your preferred icon design.
2. Each size (72 → 512px) downloads automatically as icon-NNNpx.png.
3. Rename them to icon-72.png, icon-96.png, …, icon-512.png.
4. Place them all inside frontend/icons/ folder.
5. Your PWA will now be installable with a proper icon.

Sizes needed: 72 · 96 · 128 · 144 · 152 · 192 · 384 · 512
